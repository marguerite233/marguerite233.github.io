长难句解析

发表于 2018-05-31 | 更新于 2018-08-10

If she defines feminist criticism as objective and scientific–a valid, verifiable, intellectual method that anyone, whether man or woman, can perform–the definition（代指前面黑体部分） not only precludes the critic-as-artist approach, but may, also impede accomplishment of the utilitarian political objectives of those {who seek to change the academic establishment and its thinking especially about sex role.}

In spite of such omissions, {what Rosenblatt does include(强调句) in his discussion} makes for an astute and worthwhile study.

They were fighting, albeit discreeting, to open the intellectual world to the new science and to liberate intellectual life from ecclesiastical philosophy and envisioned their work as contributing to the growth ,not of philosophy, but of research in mathematics and physics.

They

  • were fighting
    • to open the intellectual world
      • to the new science
    • to liberate intellectual life
      • from ecclesiastical philosophy
  • envisioned
    • their work
      • as contributing to the growth
        • of philosophy (X)
        • of research in mathematics and physics (O)

他们在战斗着，尽管是谨慎的，来打开学术界通向新科学的大门，以及去把学术生活从宗教哲学中解脱出来；而且他们把工作看做是对数学物理的成长，而不是对哲学研究的成长的贡献。

Although fiction assuredly springs from political circumstances, its authors react to those circumstances in ways other than ideological, and talking about novels and stories primarily as instruments of ideology circumvents much of the fictional enterprise.

  • (although) fiction
    • springs from political circumstances
  • its authors
    • react to those circumstances
      • in ways other than ideological
  • talking about novels and stories primarily as instruments of ideology
    • circumvents much of the fictional enterprise

Once we recognize this dual vision, we can accept that the play’s ironic nuances as deliberate social commentaries by Hansberry rather than as the “unintentional” irony that Bigsby attributes to the work.

一旦我们认识到这种双重观点，我们就会认为这部戏讽刺的微妙之处是Hansberry有意为之的社会评论，而不是Bigsby“无意的”讽刺。

Net phytoplankton are more likely to exclude zooplankton than are nannoplankton.

复原该句：Net phytoplankton are more likely to exclude zooplankton than nannoplankton are (likely to exclude zooplankton).

Net phytoplankton比nannoplankton更有可能驱逐zooplankton。

类似句：Hot spots are also distinguished from other volcanoes by their lavas, which contain greater amounts of alkali metals than do those from volcanoes at plate margins.

The idea of an autonomous discipline called “philosophy”, distinct from and sitting in judgement on such pursuits as theology and science turns out, on close examination, to be of quite recent origin.

and 连接的是”distinct from”和”sitting in judgement on”，两短语的宾语都是”such pursuits as theology and science”。

As[1] she put it in The Common Reader, “It is safe to say that not a single law has been framed or one stone set upon another because of anything Chaucer said or wrote; and yet, as[2] we read him, we are absorbing morality at every pore.”

[1]表示“正如”
[2]表示“当……时候”

not a single law has been framed or one stone set upon another because of anything Chaucer said or wrote。

没有一部法律被制定，也没有什么高楼大厦北建立起来是因为Chaucer所写或所说。

When the object is optimal resource allocation, that combination of legal methods should be used that most nearly yields the allocation that would exist if there were no external costs resulting from allocating resources through market activity.

当目的是最优最优资源配置时，这样的法律方法的组合应该被使用——这种组合最接近产生可能存在的最优分配，如果没有通过市场活动分配资源而导致的外部的成本。

As a consequence, it may prove difficult or impossible to establish for a successful revolution a comprehensive and truthworthy picture of those who participated, or to answer even the most basic questions one might pose concerning the social origins of the insurgent.

  • it prove
    • to establish + a picture + for…
    • to answer + questions + (which) one might pose +concerning…

several things about China
